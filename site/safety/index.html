Privacy and Safety
the way you want it

Our mission is to provide a place for people to have rich private conversations with the groups that matter to them. We want everyone to feel comfortable being their True selves on our Platform - just like they do in real life. But we understand that this type of comfort is only possible with the proper safety and privacy settings in place. Keeping our community safe is an everyday priority grounded on the safety principles below.

You control who sees your content

Our thoughtful user controls let you decide who sees your content at all times.

  • Content in a thread stays in the thread You can share with all your friends in your ‘Friends’ thread. You can also share unique content with smaller groups in each of your custom threads. No one outside of any thread can see your content in a thread.
  • Your content leaves with you When you leave a thread, all of your past content is immediately removed from the thread too. You do not need to worry about any posts staying in conversations or threads where you are no longer active. And if you leave True, all of your data and content will be deleted from the app.
  • No public broadcasting in private threads When we say private conversations, we mean it. In our private threaded experience, there is no way to share posts publicly beyond your thread. You also cannot share content from one thread to another. We want to encourage original content and minimize the spread of news, misinformation, or any type of sponsored content in private threads.

It’s that simple - you control your content without complicated privacy settings and user control features.

Public Safety is a Priority

True has zero-tolerance for violence or bullying, and we are serious about removing bad actors. Our community guidelines outline the types of engagement that we support on True. We are in this together, and we need to support each other in maintaining a safe space for everyone.

We believe your business is only your business. We provide transport-level encryption for all content on True so that you feel comfortable openly sharing. But if you see content that you believe violates our community guidelines or terms of service, please report it. We review all reports, and we will take serious action on any content or person that is reported multiple times. Actions may include a warning, account suspension, or permanent removal of the content or account. More details on our reporting process can be found here.

Real Conversations with Real People

True is a place for real people to have real conversations. We want people to feel comfortable sharing their real identities, and we want everyone to feel confident that they are only engaging with people they know and trust. We will only use your profile information to confirm your identity and help your family and friends find you.

We will not monetize your data

We collect data to improve our product and user experience. We will not monetize or sell your personal data. We created True to enable a private social experience that does not exist on the larger social networks. A private experience is not possible if you are bombarded with ads, or if you cannot trust how we are using your data. Learn more about our approach to business and data collection.

Our goal is to keep evolving and improving the ways we keep our community safe and to be transparent with you along the way. For more details on True, check out our Support Page.
