Privacy Policy

Updated October 15, 2019

True is private sharing for real friends. We’re deeply committed to giving you a safe, happy place to share — a place where you’re never forced to sell your personal infor-mation to stay connected with people you love.

Your trust is extremely important to us. That’s why we’ve attempted to write this privacy policy with as little legal jargon as possible. If something is still unclear, shoot us an email. We’ll be happy to answer any questions.

If you’re in the U.S., all the information you need is in the first few pages of the docu-ment. If you’re in the EU, be sure to have a look at our GDPR section, too.
