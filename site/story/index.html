COMMUNITY GUIDELINES TRY TRUE

Bringing back a sense
of community

You know the feeling. It’s the feeling of being at a dinner party with your close friends, sharing laughs and inside jokes, and enjoying conversation that only the people in that specific group would get. It’s connecting with a sibling that lives far away, reminiscing about childhood memories and bonding over the joys of parenthood. Or maybe it’s talking football all season with your buddies that are just as obsessed as you are. Some of the best moments in life are conversations and connections with the people that matter to us, and these are the types of moments that inspired us to build true.

We wanted to build a platform where our online conversations can mirror the feeling that we get from real life conversations, but our real life relationships are not one-dimensional. We all belong to different communities where we share different parts of ourselves. The feeling that we get from these discrete, real-life moments inspired us to build a True – a company focused on building and strengthening people’s sense of community.

Existing messaging services are not organized for groups, and they are not designed to help people capture memories or communicate effectively. And while large social networks have some group functions, they are often cluttered by noise from ads and content from people you do not know.

On True, we put people first. We focus on the quality of interactions, rather than the quantity, so that people can experience the feeling of true connection in a digital format. We give people a place where they can be their true self - with no ads, no data-tracking, and no filters - just real conversations and original content.
